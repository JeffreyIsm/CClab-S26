Imperfect Football

Project Description

Football today is imperfect. Players make decisions in the moment, and mistakes are part of the game. Nothing is fixed, and anything can happen. That unpredictability is what makes it exciting.

This project imagines a future where the sport becomes fully digitized and dominated by technology. Human players are replaced by robots, and every move is calculated with precision. In this world, the game loses its uncertainty. Everything becomes predictable, optimized, and nearly flawless.

As a human player in that world, we can no longer compete. When everything is perfect, there is no space left for imperfection, and no chance to score.
