Trinetic

Scientific Name:

Magnetic Triangle

Discovered by / at:

Discovered Inside volcano magma cells

Family:

Unknown creature found in volcano lava cells

Habitat:

Contained in an elastic bubble

Appearance:

Changing-Triangle with magnetic balls that circles around it

Key Characteristics:

  • Triangle, but never the same shape.
  • Has balls protecting it, but the amount of balls varies.
  • Tries to exit the bubble, but is protected by the bubble. If it escapes, it will die.
  • Changes color to show that it is alerted.

What we know so far about Trinetic? The creature does not like us being anywhere near it. It is always moving around and never stays still. It looks like it is trying to escape, but we don't know why it is trying to do that if it will die.
Guidelines for human interaction with this creatureClicking anywhere around the creature will make the creature aggressively spin the balls faster, as a defense mechanism to protect it from being attacked. The amount of balls changes after every click. Hovering over the creature will change its color, most likely because it is alerted.
